Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the E-PICSA Mobile Application. The E-PICSA project is committed to protecting your privacy while providing essential climate and agricultural advisory services.

2. Information We Collect

No Login Required: The E-PICSA app is designed to be used anonymously. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personally identifiable information (PII) to access the core functionality of the app.

While the app does not require PII, certain optional features may request specific permissions:

  • Location Data: The app may request access to your device's location to identify the nearest weather station, providing you with the most accurate, localized climate data. This location data is not stored or sent off-device. Once the nearest station is identified, only the station reference is stored locally.
  • Camera and Filesystem Access: If you choose to complete optional monitoring forms, you may be asked for permission to access your device's camera or filesystem to attach photos or documents.
  • Log Data and App Performance: In the case of an error, we collect anonymous log data (e.g., device model, OS version, app configuration) to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is shared.

3. Data Storage and Security

Offline Data & Local Storage: The E-PICSA app is built with an offline-first architecture. This means that your application data, settings, and tool inputs are stored securely within your device's internal memory (local databases), rather than relying on web "cookies". This ensures the app remains fully functional even without an internet connection. If you uninstall the app or clear your app data, this locally stored information will be deleted.

Data Sharing (Opt-In Features): While the app retrieves public resources securely, it may also offer opt-in features to share content you create (such as budget templates) with other users. If you choose to share a template, the data is completely anonymized and assigned a generated code. It is never linked to you or any personal identity.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

The app uses third-party services that may collect information to help us provide and improve the service. These partners have access only to the data necessary to perform their tasks on our behalf:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Performance Monitoring
  • KoboToolbox (for optional monitoring form submissions)
  • Supabase (for secure data retrieval and optional anonymized data sharing)

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the technical administrator at: support@picsa.app
