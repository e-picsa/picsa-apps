1. Introduction

The E-PICSA Dashboard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our dashboard service. This service is compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our service. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you are granted access to the dashboard, we store your email address and authentication credentials securely.
  • Usage Data: We store data related to your interactions with the dashboard content management system, such as creation and modification of deployments, to ensure data integrity and audit trails.

No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to track your behavior on this dashboard. All data remains within our secure infrastructure.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the purpose of:

  • Authenticating your access to the dashboard.
  • Allowing you to manage content and deployments.
  • Maintaing the security and integrity of the system.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a robust open specific backend-as-a-service. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restriction: You can request restriction of processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email detailed below.
